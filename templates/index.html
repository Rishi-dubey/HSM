    introduction

    Hospital management system is a computer software system for managing a hospital's operation, finances, patients, and staff. It typically includes features such as patient registration, appointment scheduling, case management, billing, and human resources. A hospital management system is a computer system that helps hospital administrators to manage various aspects of their hospital. The system can help to track patient appointments, hospital admissions, bed usage, pharmacy stocks, and laboratory results. It can also help to manage the hospital's finances, including billing and payments.
